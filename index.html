IRIDIUM

Element #: 77
Atomic Mass: 192.22 u
Atomic Molar Mass: 192.22 g/mol
Ion Charge: 4+
Melting Point: 2410 degrees Celsius
Boiling Point: 4130 degrees Celsius
Density Of Solid: 22.4 g/cm^3
Atomic Radius: 136 pm
Electron Configuration: [Xe]6s^2_4f^14_5d^7

Iridium is a silvery white metal that is extremely hard, resists corrosion extremely well, and has the highest melting point of all metals (except osmium). It is always found in the metallic state, sometimes in native gold, but usually found in a natural alloy containing osmium as well as iridium (osmiridium). It is also found in gravel deposits containing platinum ores.
